Education is not filling a bucket
but lighting a fire. ---William Yeats

I obtained my Ph.D. at Department of Computer Science, Louisiana State University, under the supervision of Dr.Feng Chen. During my PhD study, my research work mainly focuses on emerging storage technologies, such as flash memory, 3D XPoint, persistent memory, and key-value systems, etc. I received my B.S. degree at the College of Mathematics, Jilin University in 2013.

I will join Arm at Austin as a Senior Performance Engineer in June 2020.
